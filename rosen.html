During my time at Rosen Aviation, I have been developing and sustaining software in an Android/Linux based ecosystem with an increased focus on agile development in a waterfall environment. There is a broad spectrum of project experiences from embedded firmware improving our customer's experience to large scale applications increasing internal efficiencies. Since December 2021, I have also been the leader of the Rosen Green Team (Sustainability Committee). When I first started, a phrase was constantly thrown at me to reference what I was undertaking: "drinking from the fire hose." In the beginning, I don't think it really felt that way. It just felt like you do more because working full time as an engineer is a lot different from school and there is a lot to learn. Looking back, however, it is clear we cover a very broad range of responsibilities for a team of only five software engineers. That is something I am proud of, and I know that will help me as I navigate my career.
