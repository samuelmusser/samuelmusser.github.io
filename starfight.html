Language: C
Peripherals: SPI, I2C
Hardware: Nokia 5110 LCD, MCUXpresso LPC1769, serial bus, piezzo, switches

Main features include a display utilizing the Nokia 5110 graphical LCD with SPI interfacing to the subsystem of the MCUXpresso LPC1769, a custom controller with a serial interface utilizing the I2C subsystem of the LPC, an 18 note soundtrack playing "not imperial march", and sound effects through the piezo and frequency manipulation. Single player mode is an endless runner, wherein the "not tiefighter" dodges incoming laser fire. Two player mode utilizes the second set of inputs through the serial bus to allow both players to try and shoot each other and dodge their opponent's fire. Lasers shot at a time had to be limited to allow for manueverability, but also to prevent too much work from overwhelming the processor.
