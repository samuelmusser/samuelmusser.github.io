Sam Musser is a software engineer with an educational background in computer engineering. His strengths feature embedded circuit design and a broad spectrum of software engineering practices. His practical experience has led him to developing firmware featuring C in low level Linux environments and microcontrollers, high level applications featuring C# in Windows applications with Visual Studio, and Java/C++ applications in the Android Open-Source Project. What seperates him from other software/computer engineers is his capacity to have a positive influence on others and be steadfast for his team as a source of strength and positivity. Keeping things constructive, fun, and creative are what he does best when executing his technical skills. His short and long term goals coincide as he continues to navigate through the professional world where he strives to seek out meaningful progress. Sam seeks to help others in their lives by working on projects that make their lives easier and/or bring them joy, and there isn't a better place to follow his bliss technically and creatively than in the world of computer engineering.
